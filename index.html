About

Holding a PhD in Statistics, I have a strong track record of delivering insightful analysis, designing surveys, and crafting compelling reports for sectors such as healthcare, real estate, education, and marketing. In addition to my academic career, I have completed over 500+ successful freelance projects on platforms like Fiverr, specializing in hospital data analysis, medical and educational surveys, real estate, HR surveys, and product marketing research.
